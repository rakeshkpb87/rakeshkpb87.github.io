Current Research

⭐ CRISPR-based Genome Editing

Developing high-efficiency genome editing approaches to improve resistance against rust diseases. My work includes CRISPR/Cas9, transgene-free editing, and tissue–culture–free transformations.

⭐ Molecular Plant–Pathogen Interactions

Investigating host immunity pathways and engineering NLR immune receptors such as Sr50. Focus areas include effector-triggered immunity, lesion-mimic mutants, and disease resistance.

⭐ Single-Cell Transcriptomics

Using scRNA-seq to identify cell-type–specific immune regulators, transcription factors, and signaling networks.

⭐ Plant Stress Signaling

Exploring brassinosteroid signaling (e.g., DWF4), aquaporin-mediated water transport, and nutrient perception pathways.
