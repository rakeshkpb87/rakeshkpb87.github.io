Welcome

I am a plant biotechnology researcher specializing in CRISPR-based genome editing and molecular plant–pathogen interactions. I have extensive experience in CRISPR-Cas genome editing, plant transformation, and functional genomics.

My research focuses on improving disease resistance, understanding plant molecular responses, and developing innovative genome engineering approaches for crop improvement.
